How To Pray The Rosary

1. Sign of the cross, Come Holy Spirit, I Confess, Apostles' Creed


2. Our Father


3. 3 Hail Marys


4. Glory Be, Fatima Prayer


5. First Mystery, Our Father


6. 10 Hail Marys


7. Glory Be, Fatima Prayer


8. Second Mystery, Our Father. Repeat 6, 7, and 8 for the third fourth and fifth mysteries.


9. Glory Be, Fatima Paryer, Hail Holy Queen, Litany of the Blessed Virgin Mary (optional), Prayer to St. Michael the Arcangel.

End with the sign of the cross on the cross.
